E-Commerce Live Auction Feature

Core Product Owner & Lead UI/UX Designer

Product Vision

A highly engaging, round-based live auction feature designed for a consumer e-commerce platform. Instead of traditional highest-bidder-wins mechanics, this system utilizes a gamified 'bidding turns' economy. It rewards speed and platform engagement by granting the round win to the earliest bidder, transforming the standard transactional shopping experience into an interactive, high-retention event.

The User & The Problem

Target User:

Deal-seeking mobile shoppers highly responsive to gamification, daily rewards, and competitive social mechanics.

The Problem:

Standard e-commerce apps struggle with session length and daily retention. We needed a sticky, recurring feature to drive daily app opens and prolonged engagement.

Strategic Business Goals

  • Drive DAU & Session Length:

    Keep users continuously engaged during live countdowns.

  • Monetize Attention:

    Offset discount costs by routing users to an "Earn More Turns" hub (ad views, sponsored browsing).

  • Create Urgency:

    Utilize real-time status bars and push notifications to drive immediate conversion.

Core Product Mechanics

Round-Based Progression

Real-time, timed rounds with fixed price steps.

Speed-to-Win

Bids are timestamped; the earliest bidder wins the round.

Gamified Economy

Bidding costs a "turn," not immediate cash.

Growth Loops

Users earn turns via high-value actions (ads, sharing).

Dynamic Completion

Auto-closes early if multiple rounds pass with zero bids.
